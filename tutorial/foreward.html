Foreward

Fulfilling destiny is not a destination. It is an assignment, a vision, a mission, a dream, a God ordained purpose for everyone on the face of the earth. Purpose is not something you decide, it is something you discover. The discovery of which will find you either running with a vision, burning with passion, pursuing a dream or going aggressively on a mission.
My path crossed with Joe Attah a few years ago whilst he was passionately and diligently pursuing his God ordained assignment of writing books for young people. Joe is abundantly qualified to write about purpose, because it is only a man living in purpose and driven by purpose that can tell others about purpose. These few years, I have come to know the author better. The steam, passion, drive, energy and sacrifice that purpose demands from a person are consistently active in all that he does.
I want to strongly recommend this book to all young people out there. Follow the stories contained therein. Let your eyes be open to the secrets they hold. Allow the pages of this book to fan the embers of your soul until the fire of destiny in you come aglow. Your future will open up in glory and splendor as you discover, understand and pursue your God ordained purpose. See you at the top.

Sunday Ogidigbo
Lead Pastor
Holyhill Church, Abuja
