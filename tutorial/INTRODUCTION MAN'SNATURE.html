INTRODUCTION


There is no love compared to the love of our heavenly Father, the Maker of heaven, earth and everything underneath the earth. The scripture confirms His immeasurable love towards us by saying: while we were yet sinners His only begotten Son died for our sins. He does not condemn us in our sinful state. He has a way of bringing us closer to Him. His arms are always open. If God was a man, what would have been the outcome of man's existence?
Despite all the pains and rejection that you might feel when you sit back to think inwardly, you will find out that God has always been there to see you through them all. God's gift of life, hope and joy sees you through them all. He is always there to comfort us in the midst of our pains, struggles and He answers all our prayers. God is forever faithful. The only One that binds the brokenhearted and answers all their questions.
This book is a product of several emotional breakdowns I experienced with different individuals. In December 2017, my maid left suddenly without a proper goodbye despite having planned our travelling together. It was really shocking because there was no misunderstanding or any signal that she was going to leave.
This incident made me realize how unpredictable and unreliable man can be. Sometimes, I ask myself questions like why do I experience surprises, disappointment and heartbreaks from people. I once had this belief that once one is saved, one is forever guided from unpleasant happenings. The Holy Spirit answered me through following that:

1)  I expect too much from people.
2)  My service is not completely to God but also to man.
3)  My vulnerability makes people have full access to me.
4)  I sometimes put my trust in man
