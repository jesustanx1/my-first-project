CHAPTER 1

LEKKA'S STORY

It was a cold evening, the north-east trade wind blew over their faces and with his usual husky voice Kalunga said, “it is particularly windy this year” as he mentioned to what was left of his large family over dinner around the fire. This is the only month in the year when the rest of my family is complete, and this year it is particularly important because the last three of my sons have all completed their manhood trainings. He looked round at his sons, adjusted his hat on his head and said, After many years of dutiful learning and searching, you have proved yourselves to be worthy of your own responsibility to keep this kingdom in her place of pride amongst kingdoms. It is now time to determine where you belong and like your older siblings before you take your place among contributors to the wellbeing of this land.
In his characteristic manner, he began his usual long speech on special days like this, my sons, he said, the world is a gift to us but in the real sense we owe the world a gift, and that gift is inside us. It’s amazing how we fit into certain roles like they were tailor made for us. It is time my sons, to find that niche and fill it, and of course it is my duty as your father to show you that path. I have known you boys from when you were born. I know your strengths, talents, potentials, abilities and you know them too, the next few months will give us time to determine your perfect fit and you can’t miss it. Tomorrow we will begin our search, thank you boys and have a good night.
Early the next morning, Kalunga gathered his three sons together and with their bags packed, he told them they were embarking on a long journey and it could take weeks. Malo the oldest of the three asked where it was they were headed and Kalunga replied, “I will tell you as we proceed on this journey, and you will find your destination when you get there. You will know it, so just hang on”. And so the journey started and for days all they did was cover grounds and more grounds, camped for the nights and continued in the morning and as they travelled, Kalunga told his sons the tales of the different places they passed through, and then suddenly they approached a vast unkept land that had surprisingly green vegetation even though it was harmattan season. All along their trail, the grasses had withered away, but this land was surprisingly green.  Lekka, the youngest of the boys screamed! “This is awesome”. The other boys asked what was amusing him and he showed them the beauty of the land but they didn’t seem to understand. He tried to explain to them how the land could be transformed to a huge farm and everything a farm can produce could come out of this place all year round. He kept on talking for minutes as they rode on their horses and Kalunga, their father listened as the boys argued on how potent the land was but Lekka the youngest son wouldn’t let go on the issue of the land.  Just as they approached a bend on the narrow road on which they rode, they came across a brook of water, gushing out from a rock; a natural spring. Suddenly, Kalunga in his husky voice interrupted his son and announced in a loud voice, “Lekka! And he turned around posing like a knight on horse, and “said we have come to the end of your journey, to everyone on earth is given a time and space, to everyone on earth is given the ability to make his or her time productive on the space you are given. Lekka, today your passion has spoken and today your assignment is defined. You will face challenges and surmount them. You will plow lands and crops will grow. You will have cattle of different species and you will feed tribes and rescue the hungry. Your name will give hope as your barn will chase famine from many lands and put a smile on many faces. On and on he continued until he was done and finally, he said to Lekka, “don’t ever forget I am always with you, I am the Lord and Master of my lands and estates and will be there whenever you need me, to help you all the way to success. So call out to me when you need anything and I will be right there. Now go out there and do your thing.” Then Kalunga turned to Malo and Pember and told them in a stern voice that they had to continue on their journey immediately as their different destinations awaited them. They set out immediately, and bade Lekka farewell and continued on their way.
This story illustrates how we find our passion and our assignment when we build our relationship with God, and walk with Him and take trips with Him like Kalunga and his sons. When we do all these, we would then discover what our role is in time. A relationship with God makes it possible to know where your passion and assignment lies, because God is the source. Everything starts with God, runs with God and ends with God. Like Kalunga’s sons, they always had access to their father for guidance and directions, the same is true of God our father. He is always there for us to guide, protect and direct us on every step of the way on our journey to fulfill God’s purpose.

Your Passion

These two words form one of the strongest phrases I know, and can inform and reform the mind of anyone seeking fulfillment. It’s amazing how God wired every human to have a passion for something and how that passion lives and grows within us from child-hood to adult-hood. Passion is often described as a strong feeling of enthusiasm or excitement about something or about doing something. Passion is the reason why you hear people say that, “I have always wanted to do this from when I was a kid” or they say, “I have always had this at the back of my mind”. At the same time, I know someone out there is saying, “I don’t really connect to anything strong enough and I am not so sure I have a passion for anything”. “Some other person may say I think I have a passion for this and that but I am not always sure”. Firstly, we need to know what passion is and what passion is not. Most people readily assume their talents, gifts and abilities should automatically determine where their passion lies. Well these are usually pointers and sometimes appears so but that is not usually the case because it will mean every 6 foot tall person should be a basketball Player or every brilliant student should study medicine or because you can paint well you should be an artist, all that could be true but that does not necessarily guarantee fulfillment. You may not be satisfied or fulfilled after making those decisions just because of your ability to do them. You could be 6 feet tall and love to cook more than anything in the world and eventually become a world class chef. Below are some questions that can help you sieve your thoughts;

  • How much effort have you deliberately put into finding out what your passion might be? Without a deliberate search it is sometimes difficult to recognize even though it’s right inside you
  • How much time have you spent praying about where your passion lies? It’s God’s idea and He is usually happy to hear you ask.
  • What do you enjoy doing even if you are not paid?
  •  What grieves you and you don’t like to see

Every person out there that really wants to be successful must set apart time for him/herself to find out where his or her passion lies. Even though there is no specific time frame that you can fix to determine what your passion is, it is not a futile pursuit, trust me, it is a question God loves to answer and you will enjoy the process if you will set quality time apart to find out. This is because your passion is God’s signature on your soul and he will not deprive a searching heart the answer as it’s His idea in the first place that you come to that awareness.  The day you start asking God about what your passion is, that day God starts feeling a sense of accomplishment about you. This is because soon you will be able to see his plan for you. The suggestions below will help you appreciate your passion clearly

  • You need to acknowledge that God created you with a passion for something that will help improve humanity.
  • Secondly you do not have to stereotype your thoughts to think that you should possess a particular passion you know or think
  • Your passion does not have to tally with a profession just yet. It might just be a desire too broad to capture in an occupation.
  • You don’t choose your passion it’s right inside you and has some kinetic energy flowing in you, just identify with it.
  • Passion will instigate you toward a crusade, campaign, a profession or a practice.

Passion makes you feel part of something beyond you, Passion is not selfish. For example, if I say I have passion for shoes, this could mean I love making or designing shoes but not buying every shoe you see or having so many shoes that you can’t wear and saying, oh I just love shoes and that’s my passion. That won’t exactly be Passion for shoes. It should include solving a problem around or making more shoes available.

It is also important to know that our passion does not always come as jobs or titles. Let’s take some examples. If you hate to see sick people hurting and you have a passion to help them; that is great. But this does not directly translate into being a doctor, this is because you just may not be wired to like chemistry and biology and the sciences to see you through medical school. On the other hand you might love literature and government and the arts, but this does not change the fact that you have a passion to help hurting and sick people, all you need to do is to find out how you can use what you are wired for to achieve the same thing and make your contributions in that direction, you might work in government to provide policies or in an Non Governmental Organization to raise funds for the treatment and welfare of this group of people. It could come up in a number of ways. Acknowledging where your passion lies helps to direct your path and vocation. It makes more meaning when your occupation is in line with your passion and that should be your target.
It is important to note that our passion opens us up to the world we will love to see but our talents, potential and abilities as well as the opportunities in our immediate environment help to guide our energies to achieving something worthwhile. Your passion is not the solution to the problems of the world, but it creates an environment for your assignment to incubate and mature.
How do you know that what you are suspecting in your heart may be your passion?
Please find below some important questions you may want to consider

  • Does it seek to improve a situation and make it better?
  • Does it seek to eradicate an unpleasant situation?
  • Does it help to multiply and sustain something that is good and already working?
  • Is it about others, humanity and not just you?

Your Talents

Your talents, gifts and abilities are inborn. They are natural endowments that you possess and you can use with little or no training. These are God given abilities and skills that make you achieve or solve problems effortlessly. Your unique ability, gifts or talents are given to you for a purpose. They are strong indicators to the solution of the problem God called you to solve either by rendering a service, deploying a skill or managing a project. Your passion easily fuels your talent, gifts and abilities. Your talents, gifts and abilities may not ultimately be your assignment but the medium that will give you access to the problem you are created to solve. In excising your talents, gifts and ability you develop capacity to own a skill and build on it. This makes you proficient and confident and helps to provide a direction for your life. Your talents, gifts and abilities may be artistic, intelligence based or may require physical strength. God expects you to develop your gifts and talents and make the most of it. The reason he gave you that ability is not just so you can make money, have a great life and then die, the reason he gave you that ability is so that your success can benefit others and bring glory to Him. Remember everything you enjoy and cannot produce is a product of another person’s talent or gift, if we all kept our talents to ourselves then, we would have to make a car to own one, sew a shirt to wear one. So desire to make the impact of your talent felt by others. If you are not so sure what your talents are, please, find below some important points and questions you may want to consider:

  • What things do you do with ease
  • Things you can easily do without much practice
  • Ask questions from your close friends and family
  • Evaluate your past successes
  • Try new things from time to time

Your Dreams

The word dream is dynamic. It is used in various ways to explain different experiences. However, the basic concept of a dream revolves around mental pictures of expectations that stir up actions to see the fulfillment of those pictures and thoughts. The reason why the word dream is used in different ways is largely because of how these mental pictures are conceived in the mind of the dreamer.
Three common sources of dreams

  • Dreams can be a series of thoughts, images or feelings that occur in your mind when you are asleep and I believe this comes from God. Sometimes they may occur a number of times or just once but the images and thoughts remain vivid and linger for a long time, sometimes even for a life time.
  • Dreams also come from thinking and reflecting on a particular issue over a period of time while you are awake, it usually involves your imagination and sometimes meditation to have a fully formed picture.
  • Dreams can also be born out of a cherished ambition or strong desire to become or achieve something. This is sometimes inspired by elements in the environment, like family background or other peculiar experiences growing up amongst other reasons.

Regardless of how the dreams come to you, they belong to you and that is God’s way of communicating an assignment to you. That is why the mental images and pictures will keep roaming around your mind until you give it a practical attention.
Dreams are powerful and it is one of the ways that God communicates His plans to man. Dreams are personal and have a supernatural ability to empower the dreamer. Dreams have the power to fuel your imagination and propel you to think and create the reality. Dreams reveal the details of the future and help us to see the possibilities in achieving what we see.
Some characteristics of Dreams

  • A dream makes your assignment clearer.
  • Dreams motivate you to sharpen your talents as you now know what to use it for.
  • Dreams inspire you not to give up when challenges arise.

Life Play

Basically, your passion, talents and dreams are key indicators to what direction your life is heading and what problem you have been created to solve. It is important to note that you do not need to know these three indicators all at once or in any particular order to fulfill your assignment. They are interwoven and as you begin to make progress with any of them that you have discovered the rest will naturally find expression. So on your quest to fulfilling you assignment and understanding these key indicators you need to prayerfully commune with God to lead and direct you.
