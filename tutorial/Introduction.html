INTRODUCTION

 

GOD'S BIG PICTURE

The most important thing about living on earth is knowing why you are here and God is the only one who can tell you why you are here because he made you. Let us look at it critically from the beginning.  First of all, it will interest you to know that what you call your future is an agenda designed by God to bring glory to his name. However, this agenda is also designed to honor you in the process but it is up to you to decide to accept God’s agenda or not. I think it will be a smart move to decide to follow his agenda because God created the agenda. God created the agenda before he created you. Before God created you in the womb he knew you and created an assignment for you to do. In fact it was because of the assignment he created you custom built to fit that assignment and then God wired you with gifts and talents and the passion that will help you accomplish that assignment. So, everything you need has always been inside you from the beginning. When you know this it becomes easier to trust God with your Future.

  • Firstly, you have to acknowledge that God is the source of your life and owner of the future you desire. God created you for a purpose and there is something God will like you to specifically do on His behalf while you are on earth.
  • Secondly, you need to know that your assignment is God’s idea and not yours. It is very important to Him that you recognize that you are His representative or ambassador standing in for Him to carry out His plan. In other words, you don’t choose your assignment you discover it.
  • Thirdly, everything you need to know about this awesome plan that will make your life colorful is tied to a functional relationship with God.

We will discuss three important things that can suggest to you or help you discover God’s plan for your life. This is important because everything you need to make your life colourful is embedded in your assignment. These three variables hold clues and sometimes serve as indicators or pointers to who God made you to be or what he wants you to do. Other times, these clues only serve as avenues or channels that point you to the problem you are designed to solve.
